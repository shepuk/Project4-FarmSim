What is Sunrise Farm?

Sunrise Farm is an interactive farm game/simulation.

As a new farm owner, you can grow and harvest crops on a patch of farm land. Buying and selling crops is also an important part of your duties.

Your farm

Head over to your farm and you will see a crop patch and 8 slots in which you can plant seeds. Select a seed from the dropdown menu (you may need to buy some first!) and click it to plant that seed. A timer will then show, and this is your plant's countdown to harvest time.

Once your seed is ready, you may harvest it by clicking the 'harvest' button underneath. You'll get a new item in your inventory depending on what you planted. For example, if you plant a carrot seed, you can expect a carrot to be harvested and added to your inventory.
